Sport

Of course, there's also the obvious health benefit of fitness and sanity. I've always been interested in sports, from watching rugby matches on the TV to travelling to foreign countries to perform at festivals. Over many years I have tried a variety of sports including:

Boxing

Irish Dancing

My favourite of all, boxing, has been the most rewarding but challenging.

I began it in 4th years as part of Gaisce presidential award, and I'm glad I did.

The fitness aspect is very beneficial as training consists of an hour of running, skipping and HIT training. It also equips me with important self defence skills		 Irish Dancing has been my main sport for many years now.

I began when I was 4 and have loved it ever since. The most competitive years came in 2016 and 2017 where I competed at the Irish Dancing World Championships in Glasgow and Dublin.
